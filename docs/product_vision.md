# Product Vision

PsyBalance is an asynchronous coach + habit tracker for behavior change.
The product is intentionally narrow: it helps a human coach work with a client
through lightweight signals, not through continuous therapy-like automation.

## What the product is for

- low-friction daily behavior tracking
- coach-guided support in between sessions
- sustainable engagement without pressure
- behavior-first coaching, not diet ideology

## Why the product exists

The core hypothesis in the project spec is that sustainable change usually fails
because behavior is left to autopilot:

- nutrition
- sleep
- stress
- activity
- relationships

PsyBalance exists to make those signals visible and coachable with minimal friction.

## Business context

The MVP was designed around an async coaching subscription model.
The original product spec uses a €60/month target price with an 80/20 split
between coach and platform.

That business shape matters because it forces the product to be:

- cheap to operate
- explainable to coaches
- resilient to sparse engagement
- useful without real-time human presence

## What the product is not

- not autonomous therapy
- not a mental health diagnosis system
- not a manipulative engagement loop
- not a generic social app
- not a broad enterprise health platform

## Product-level tradeoff

The product deliberately accepts a smaller feature set in exchange for clarity:

- fewer flows
- fewer hidden heuristics
- stronger explainability
- stronger coach judgment
- lower risk of behavioral overreach

That tradeoff is intentional and should remain visible in future changes.
